# PBC list — provided-by-client schedule

The PBC ("provided by client") or "prepared by client" list is the
single most-leveraged workpaper in the engagement. It governs
client expectations, fieldwork sequencing, and the engagement
timeline. Send 3–6 weeks before fieldwork start; refresh weekly
during fieldwork.

Each PBC item carries: `index` (workpaper cross-ref) | `description`
| `period` | `requested_date` | `requested_by` | `received_date`
| `received_by` | `WP_index` (where the item lands in the file) |
`status` (open / received / not-applicable / withdrawn).

## Standard PBC list (every engagement)

### Permanent / one-time items
- `[ ]` Articles of incorporation / formation; current bylaws or
  operating agreement; partnership agreement (if applicable).
- `[ ]` Recent board / shareholder / member meeting minutes.
- `[ ]` Current organizational chart.
- `[ ]` Current legal counsel listing + outside-counsel inquiry
  list (for legal-letter request, audit only).
- `[ ]` Current insurance schedule (D&O, E&O, property, GL,
  workers' comp, cyber).
- `[ ]` Loan and lease agreements (debt schedule + ASC 842 lease
  inventory).
- `[ ]` Material contracts: customer master agreements, vendor
  master agreements, employment agreements, IP licenses, real-
  estate leases.
- `[ ]` Buy-sell agreement, ESOP plan, deferred-compensation plan,
  stock-option plan documents.
- `[ ]` Latest tax returns (federal, state, local, foreign).
- `[ ]` Prior-year audit / review / compilation report and
  workpapers (if predecessor; contingent on AU-C §510 access).

### Trial balance / GL
- `[ ]` Period-end trial balance (TB) — both adjusted and
  unadjusted, in the firm's preferred format (Excel preferred,
  with subtotals by FS line).
- `[ ]` Period-end general ledger detail by account.
- `[ ]` Chart of accounts with FS-line mapping.
- `[ ]` Journal-entry register for the period (every JE; for
  audits, review unusual JEs per AU-C §240).
- `[ ]` Adjusting journal entries (AJEs) since prior-period close.

### Cash and equivalents
- `[ ]` Period-end bank reconciliations for every account, with
  bank statements attached.
- `[ ]` Year-end bank statements covering the cutoff period
  (typically 30–60 days post-period-end).
- `[ ]` Bank confirmation request forms (for audits — use AICPA
  standard form or equivalent).
- `[ ]` Schedule of restricted cash, escrow, sweep accounts.
- `[ ]` Outstanding-check listing.

### Accounts receivable
- `[ ]` AR aging at period-end (current, 30, 60, 90, > 90).
- `[ ]` Subsequent collections through fieldwork date.
- `[ ]` Allowance-for-credit-losses calculation (ASC 326 if CECL
  applies; ASC 310 for legacy; route to `research-financial-
  reporting` for ASC 326 issues).
- `[ ]` AR confirmation positive/negative selection (audits).
- `[ ]` Customer-deposit / unearned-revenue schedule.

### Inventory
- `[ ]` Period-end inventory listing by location.
- `[ ]` Inventory-counting procedures + count sheets.
- `[ ]` Inventory cost methodology (FIFO / weighted avg / specific
  ID); standard-cost variances if applicable.
- `[ ]` Lower-of-cost-or-NRV (LCNRV) analysis (ASC 330).
- `[ ]` Slow-moving / obsolete inventory schedule.
- `[ ]` Consignment-in / consignment-out detail.

### Fixed assets
- `[ ]` Fixed-asset rollforward: beginning balance + additions +
  disposals + transfers = ending; same for accumulated
  depreciation.
- `[ ]` Capital-expenditure detail with invoices for material
  additions.
- `[ ]` Depreciation schedule with method, useful life,
  in-service date.
- `[ ]` Disposal listing with proceeds + gain/loss calc.
- `[ ]` ASC 360 impairment indicators screen.

### Leases (ASC 842)
- `[ ]` Lease inventory: lessor, asset description, commencement,
  term, payments, options.
- `[ ]` ROU asset and lease-liability rollforward.
- `[ ]` Discount-rate documentation (incremental borrowing rate
  methodology).
- `[ ]` Practical expedients elected.
- (Route to `research-asc-606-842` for ASC 842 substantive issues.)

### Investments / intangibles
- `[ ]` Investment statements; investment policy.
- `[ ]` Goodwill rollforward + ASC 350 impairment screen.
- `[ ]` Intangible asset rollforward + ASC 350 impairment screen.

### Accounts payable / accrued liabilities
- `[ ]` AP aging + subsequent payments.
- `[ ]` Cutoff sample of subsequent disbursements (search for
  unrecorded liabilities — audits).
- `[ ]` Accrued payroll and benefits computation.
- `[ ]` Accrued bonus / commission computation with documentation
  of management approval.
- `[ ]` Accrued vacation / PTO calculation.
- `[ ]` Tax accruals: federal, state, payroll, sales/use,
  property.

### Debt
- `[ ]` Debt schedule: principal, interest rate, maturity,
  collateral, covenants.
- `[ ]` Loan confirmation forms (audits).
- `[ ]` Covenant compliance computations + waivers if applicable.
- `[ ]` Subsequent debt activity (advances, paydowns,
  refinancings).

### Equity
- `[ ]` Equity rollforward: beginning + net income + distributions
  + contributions + other = ending.
- `[ ]` Ownership schedule with capital accounts (partnerships /
  LLCs).
- `[ ]` Stock register / membership register; option-grant
  detail.

### Income statement
- `[ ]` Revenue waterfall: contract / customer / product detail
  reconciled to GL.
- `[ ]` ASC 606 contract review for material customer
  arrangements (route to `research-asc-606-842`).
- `[ ]` Cost of revenue / cost of goods sold detail.
- `[ ]` Operating-expense detail by category, with variance
  explanation vs prior period and budget.
- `[ ]` Interest expense and capitalized interest schedule.
- `[ ]` Other income / loss detail.

### Income taxes (ASC 740)
- `[ ]` Current and deferred tax provision with state breakouts.
- `[ ]` Deferred-tax-asset / liability rollforward.
- `[ ]` Valuation-allowance analysis (route to `research-asc-740`).
- `[ ]` UTP register + ASC 740-10 measurement (route to
  `research-asc-740`).

### Related parties / contingencies / commitments
- `[ ]` Related-party transactions schedule (lease, loan,
  service).
- `[ ]` Litigation summary with outside-counsel inquiry letter
  responses.
- `[ ]` Loss-contingency assessment per ASC 450.
- `[ ]` Off-balance-sheet commitments and guarantees.

### Subsequent events
- `[ ]` Subsequent-events memo from management covering the
  period from period-end through report-release date.
- `[ ]` Updated bank reconciliations / customer collections / AP
  cutoff through fieldwork date.

### Management representation
- `[ ]` Management representation letter (audits — required per
  AU-C §580; reviews — required per AR-C §90.A102).

## Industry overlays

| Industry | Add to PBC list |
|---|---|
| Manufacturing | Standard-cost variance analysis; production capacity utilization; inventory-by-location with cycle-count results. |
| SaaS / tech | Customer contract repository; ASC 606 SSP analysis; deferred-revenue rollforward by contract; capitalized software (ASC 350-40) detail. |
| Construction | Job cost schedules; %-of-completion calc per ASC 606; surety / bonding info; backlog detail. |
| Real estate | ASC 842 lease inventory (lessor side); CAM reconciliations; ASC 970 / ASC 360 impairment; cap-rate analysis for impairment. |
| Healthcare | Net patient service revenue calc with payer-mix detail; allowance for contractual adjustments; Medicare/Medicaid cost reports. |
| Not-for-profit | Restricted-net-asset rollforward; donor-restriction detail; in-kind contributions; functional-expense allocation; UBI (Form 990-T) detail. |
| Employee-benefit plan | Census data; contribution / withdrawal detail; investment statements with cap-and-floor reconciliation; certified trustee report (DOL §103(a)(3)(C) audits — distinct from full audits per ASC and DOL); fidelity bond. |
| Governmental | Out of scope here — route to GAGAS / Yellow Book / Single Audit externally. |

## Update cadence

- **3–6 weeks pre-fieldwork:** initial PBC delivered to client.
- **2 weeks pre-fieldwork:** status check; escalate items still open.
- **1 week pre-fieldwork:** final reminder; identify items the
  client cannot deliver and escalate to engagement partner.
- **During fieldwork:** weekly PBC status; track open items and
  the staff/manager owner.
- **End of fieldwork:** every PBC item must be `received` or
  `withdrawn`; open items at fieldwork end are workpaper-file
  exceptions to be resolved before partner sign-off.

The PBC list is also the spine of the engagement-progress report
to the client and the engagement-economics tracking (hours by
in-charge / manager / partner; budget-to-actual at PBC-line
level).
