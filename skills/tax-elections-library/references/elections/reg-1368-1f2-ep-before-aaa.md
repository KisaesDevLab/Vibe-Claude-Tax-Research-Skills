## F6. Reg. §1.1368-1(f)(2) Distribute E&P Before AAA

**Authority:** Treas. Reg. §1.1368-1(f)(2); IRC §1368(e)(3).

**Purpose.** A former C corporation that has elected S status may
have **accumulated earnings and profits (AE&P)** from C corp years.
By default under §1368, S corp distributions reduce the
**Accumulated Adjustments Account (AAA)** first (tax-free to the
extent of basis), then E&P (taxable as dividend), then return of
remaining basis.

The §1.1368-1(f)(2) election reverses this ordering, allowing the
corporation to distribute **E&P FIRST** before AAA. This is
generally counterintuitive (it accelerates dividend recognition
to shareholders) but is useful when:

1. The corporation wants to **eliminate C-era E&P** to avoid future
   §1375 passive investment income tax issues. §1375 imposes a
   corporate-level tax on excess passive investment income for S
   corps with AE&P.
2. The corporation faces potential §1362(d)(3) **S election
   termination** for excessive passive investment income with AE&P
   (3 consecutive years > 25% passive investment income).
3. Shareholders are in low brackets in the current year and want to
   accelerate dividend recognition.

**Who makes the election.** The corporation, with **consent of all
affected shareholders** who received distributions during the year.

**When to file.** Attach to the timely-filed (with extensions) Form
1120-S for the year of distribution.

**Where to file.** With Form 1120-S.

**Sample election language:**

```
ELECTION & SHAREHOLDER CONSENT TO DISTRIBUTE E&P BEFORE AAA

Corporation:   ____________________________________________________
Address:       ____________________________________________________
               ____________________________________________________
FEIN:          ____________________________________________________

The above corporation hereby elects under Treas. Reg.
§1.1368-1(f)(2) to treat distributions to shareholders during
the taxable year ended December 31, ____ as first made from the
corporation's accumulated earnings & profits.

All affected shareholders who received corporate distributions
during the stated year hereby consent to this election below.

Shareholder Consents:

Stockholder: _______________________   Date: ______________
  Signed: _________________________
  Address: _______________________________________________________
  Address: _______________________________________________________

Stockholder: _______________________   Date: ______________
  Signed: _________________________
  Address: _______________________________________________________
  Address: _______________________________________________________
```

**Notes / Pitfalls.**

- All shareholders who received distributions during the year must
  consent. Failing to obtain a consent voids the election.
- Distributions treated as dividends are subject to qualified dividend
  rates (0%, 15%, or 20%) at the shareholder level, plus NIIT if
  applicable.
- Once C-era E&P is eliminated, subsequent S corp distributions are
  governed entirely by AAA / OAA / basis rules under §1368.
- Useful interaction with §1375 passive investment income tax
  planning and §1362(d)(3) S election termination avoidance.
- Combine with deemed dividend election (F7) when no actual cash
  distribution is desired but E&P elimination is.
