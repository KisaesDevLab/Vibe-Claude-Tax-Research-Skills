## Notes

- ¬ß9100 relief framework: Reg. ¬ß301.9100-1 (general); Reg.
  ¬ß301.9100-2 (automatic 12-month and 6-month extensions); Reg.
  ¬ß301.9100-3 (discretionary relief ‚Äî taxpayer must show

- Statutory elections (e.g., ¬ß83(b) 30-day) are NOT eligible for
  ¬ß9100 relief; the deadline is jurisdictional.
- Built-in relief Rev. Procs. (2009-41, 2013-30, 2022-32) provide
  taxpayer-favorable safe harbors that supersede the ¬ß9100-3
  discretionary path for the specific elections covered.
