# ASC 740-10-50 + ASU 2023-09 — Income Tax Disclosures

ASC 740-10-50 establishes income-tax disclosure requirements.
ASU 2023-09 (Income Taxes — Disclosures) substantially expands
disclosure requirements for PBE Dec. 15, 2024 effective date,
non-PBE Dec. 15, 2025.

## Standard disclosures (ASC 740-10-50)

### Components of income tax expense (ASC 740-10-50-9)
- Current expense / benefit by jurisdiction (federal, state,
  foreign).
- Deferred expense / benefit by jurisdiction.
- Investment tax credits.
- Government grants recognized as a reduction of income taxes.
- Other significant components.

### Rate reconciliation (ASC 740-10-50-12)

For PBE: tabular reconciliation of statutory federal rate (21%)
to effective rate (ETR), in dollar amounts AND percentage. ASU
2023-09 EXPANDS to:
- State income tax (net of federal benefit).
- Tax effect of foreign earnings vs. U.S. earnings.
- Federal income tax credits, by category if material.
- Permanent differences (specific categories).
- Changes in valuation allowance.
- Changes in unrecognized tax benefits (UTBs).
- Cross-border / international items (GILTI / FDII / BEAT,
  if applicable).
- Other reconciling items by category.

ASU 2023-09 imposes a quantitative threshold (5% of statutory tax
for the period) for disclosure of categories.

### Significant components of DTAs / DTLs (ASC 740-10-50-2)
- DTAs by category (NOLs, credit carryforwards, accruals,
  reserves, employee compensation, etc.).
- DTLs by category (depreciation, intangibles, undistributed
  foreign earnings, other).
- Total valuation allowance and changes during the year.

### Carryforwards (ASC 740-10-50-3)
- Approximate amount of net operating loss and credit
  carryforwards.
- Expiration dates of carryforwards.
- For carryforwards not subject to expiration (post-TCJA NOLs),
  note the indefinite life.

### UTB rollforward (ASC 740-10-50-15)

For PBE only (private companies are exempt from UTB rollforward
disclosure but must disclose UTB total and certain components):
- Beginning balance.
- Additions for current-year tax positions.
- Additions for prior-year tax positions.
- Reductions for prior-year tax positions.
- Reductions due to settlements.
- Reductions due to lapse of statute.
- Effects of foreign currency translation.
- Ending balance.

### UTB — additional disclosures (ASC 740-10-50-15A)
- Total UTB that, if recognized, would affect the effective tax
  rate.
- Total amount of accrued interest and penalties (if not
  separately classified).
- Description of tax years still subject to examination by major
  tax jurisdiction.
- Reasonable possibility of significant change in UTB within 12
  months.

### Indefinite reinvestment / outside basis (ASC 740-30-50)

For non-distributed foreign subsidiary earnings:
- If indefinite reinvestment asserted: undistributed foreign
  earnings amount and reasons for not providing for U.S. taxes.
- Hypothetical U.S. tax liability if indefinite reinvestment
  were not asserted.

### Risks and uncertainties (ASC 740-10-50-15A and 50-15B)

Tax risks and uncertainties not constituting UTBs (e.g., audit
adjustments under negotiation but recognized as more-likely-
than-not).

## ASU 2023-09 specific additions (effective dates above)

### Income taxes paid disaggregation
- Income taxes paid (net of refunds) disaggregated by:
  - Federal.
  - State (with separate disclosure of states with material
    individual amounts).
  - Foreign (with separate disclosure of jurisdictions with
    material individual amounts; "material" defined by
    quantitative threshold).

### Rate reconciliation expansion (above)

### Quantitative thresholds for disclosure
ASU 2023-09 establishes specific thresholds — see ASU 2023-09
text for current-year quantitative tests.

## Private-company disclosures

Private companies must disclose:
- Total UTB.
- Components of UTB at high level.
- Note on tax years subject to examination.

Private companies are EXEMPT from:
- UTB rollforward (PBE only).
- ASU 2023-09 expanded rate-reconciliation categories until Dec.
  15, 2025 (then required).

## Examples of useful disclosures (illustrative)

### Sample rate reconciliation
> "Statutory federal income tax rate: 21.0%
> State and local income taxes, net of federal benefit: 4.5%
> Foreign tax differential: (1.2%)
> Permanent differences: 0.8%
> Valuation allowance, net change: (0.5%)
> Tax credits: (1.0%)
> Other: 0.3%
> Effective tax rate: 23.9%"

### Sample DTA disclosure
> "Net operating loss carryforwards (federal, state): $X
> Credit carryforwards (FTC, R&D credit): $X
> Accrued expenses: $X
> Stock-based compensation: $X
> Allowance for credit losses: $X
> Other: $X
> Total deferred tax assets: $XX
> Valuation allowance: ($X)
> Net deferred tax assets: $XX"
