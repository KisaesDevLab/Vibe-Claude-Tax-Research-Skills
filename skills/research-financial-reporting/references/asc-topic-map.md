# ASC Topic Map — routing by ASC topic family

The Codification organizes guidance by topic family. Topics are
3-digit codes; subtopics are 2-digit; sections are 2-digit;
subsections are sequential. Cite as ASC TTT-SS-SS-SS (e.g., ASC
606-10-25-1).
