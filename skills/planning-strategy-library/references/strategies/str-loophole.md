# Short-Term-Rental "Loophole"

**Slug:** `str-loophole`
**Primary IRC section:** Treas. Reg. Sec. 1.469-1T(e)(3)(ii)

## Summary

STR (average customer use <= 7 days) is NOT a 'rental activity' under Sec. 469; treated as trade or business; material-participation analysis under Sec. 1.469-5T applies without REPS.

## Eligibility

- Average customer rental period <= 7 days OR <= 30 days with significant personal services.
- Material participation per Sec. 1.469-5T.
- Active management substantiated.

## Mechanics

1. Confirm average rental period via booking data.
2. Manage actively (turnover, repairs, listing optimization).
3. Maintain hour log per Sec. 1.469-5T tests (1, 3, 4 most commonly).
4. Apply Sec. 168(k) bonus + cost-segregation for major depreciation.
5. Route to `predict-real-estate-pro` for STR-specific analysis.
