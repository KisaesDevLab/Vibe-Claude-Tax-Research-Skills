# Section 453 Installment Sale

**Slug:** `installment-sale-453`
**Primary IRC section:** IRC Sec. 453

## Summary

Taxpayer sells property for deferred payments; gain recognized pro-rata as payments received under installment method.

## Eligibility

- Sale of property (NOT inventory; NOT publicly-traded securities for individuals).
- At least one payment received in a year after sale year.
- Sec. 453(b)(1) eligible.

## Mechanics

1. Document sale terms with note / installment agreement.
2. Compute gross profit ratio per Sec. 453.
3. Recognize gain pro-rata as payments received.
4. Sec. 453A interest charge if installment receivable > $5M (corporate) / $150K (individual at year-end).
5. Form 6252 reporting.
