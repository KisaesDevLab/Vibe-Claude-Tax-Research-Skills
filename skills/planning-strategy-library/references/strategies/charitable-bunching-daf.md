# Charitable Bunching with Donor-Advised Fund

**Slug:** `charitable-bunching-daf`
**Primary IRC section:** IRC Sec. 170

## Summary

Taxpayer aggregates multiple years of charitable contributions into a single year via a Donor-Advised Fund to clear the standard deduction floor; subsequent grants from DAF are non-tax events.

## Eligibility

- Itemizing in the contribution year exceeds standard deduction ($14,600 single / $29,200 MFJ 2024).
- Sufficient cash or appreciated securities available for bunched contribution.
- DAF sponsor accepts contribution.

## Mechanics

1. Open DAF at sponsor (Fidelity Charitable, Schwab Charitable, Vanguard Charitable, community foundation).
2. Contribute cash or appreciated securities; capture immediate Sec. 170 deduction (cash 60% AGI / appreciated 30% AGI).
3. Make grants from DAF over multi-year periods to qualifying charities; grants are not tax events.
4. Substantiate per Sec. 170(f)(8) (>= $250 contemporaneous written acknowledgment).
