## Schedule L — Balance Sheet

Required for partnerships with total receipts ≥ $250K AND total
assets ≥ $250K. Capital-account section reconciles to Schedule M-2.

## Schedule B — Other Information

Includes yes/no on:
- BBA opt-out under §6221(b) (if ≤ 100 partners and all eligible).
- §704(b) substantial-economic-effect declarations.
- §754 election in effect.
- §6038A foreign-owned status.
- Foreign partners.
- Nominee status and disregarded-entity reporting.

## §704(b) and §704(c)

§704(b) substantial-economic-effect: capital-account maintenance
+ liquidation per positive capital + DRO or QIO.

§704(c) tracks built-in gain/loss on contributed property.
Methods: traditional, traditional with curative, remedial.

§704(c)(1)(B) and §737 — 7-year disguised-sale-and-mixing-bowl
rules.

## BBA partnership audit regime

§6221(a) — partnership-level audit; partnership pays imputed
underpayment unless:
- §6226 push-out election (notify partners within 45 days of
  Notice of Final Partnership Adjustment).
- §6221(b) opt-out (≤ 100 partners; all eligible — not
  partnerships, not certain trusts).
- Pull-in procedure (modify partner returns for mod-amend).

Tax matters partner role replaced by Partnership Representative
(§6223). PR can be ANY person with a substantial U.S. presence;
designated annually on the return.

## Forms and schedules referenced

- Form 1125-A (COGS).
- Form 4797 (sales of business property).
- Form 8825 (rental real estate).
- Form 8990 (§163(j) at partnership level).
- Form 8865 (foreign partnership reporting — for U.S. partner
  filings, not the partnership itself).

## Computed metrics

- **Per-partner ordinary income** = Line 22 × profit %.
- **§704(c) built-in gain/loss** balances per partner.
- **§752 liability allocation** (recourse vs nonrecourse).
- **Capital-account analysis** under tax-basis method.
- **Self-employment income** (Box 14) for general partners (LLC
  members typically NOT subject to SE tax under §1402(a)(13);
  ongoing IRS scrutiny, see Soroban, T.C. Memo. 2023-129).

## Common practitioner pull-through items

- §704(b) substantial-economic-effect compliance.
- §754 election analysis when partner sells / dies.
- §752 liability allocation accuracy.
- §163(j) at partnership level + EBIE allocation.
- BBA opt-out election eligibility.
- §6223 Partnership Representative designation.
- Self-employment vs limited-partner classification (Soroban
  pattern).
