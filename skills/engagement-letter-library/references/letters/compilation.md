# Compilation engagement letter (AR-C §80.10)

Required elements per AR-C §60.A21 + AR-C §80.10. Lack-of-
independence path available with required disclosure.

## Required AICPA elements

- [ ] Statement that engagement is to compile F/S in accordance
      with SSARS.
- [ ] Statement that engagement does NOT contemplate obtaining or
      providing assurance.
- [ ] Statement that practitioner is NOT required to verify
      accuracy or completeness.
- [ ] If lack of independence: disclosure that firm is not
      independent.
- [ ] Identification of framework (GAAP, tax-basis, cash-basis,
      regulatory-basis, contractual-basis, other).
- [ ] Form of compilation report.
- [ ] Going-concern consideration if conditions arise.
- [ ] Management responsibilities.
- [ ] Fee, termination, communication terms.

## Template

> **COMPILATION ENGAGEMENT LETTER**
>
> [DATE]
>
> [CLIENT NAME]
> [CLIENT ADDRESS]
>
> We are pleased to confirm our understanding of the terms of
> our engagement to compile the financial statements of [CLIENT
> NAME] for the [PERIOD/YEAR] ending [DATE].
>
> **Engagement**
>
> Our engagement is to compile financial statements that comprise
> [LIST: balance sheet / income statement / etc.] in accordance
> with [framework: U.S. GAAP / tax-basis / cash-basis / regulatory-
> basis / contractual-basis / other].
>
> Our engagement will be performed in accordance with Statements
> on Standards for Accounting and Review Services (SSARS) issued
> by the AICPA. The engagement does not contemplate obtaining or
> providing any assurance. We are not required to perform
> procedures to verify the accuracy or completeness of the
> information provided by management.
>
> **Independence** [SELECT ONE]
>
> [(a) WE ARE INDEPENDENT:] We are independent of [Client] in
> accordance with the AICPA Code of Professional Conduct.
>
> [(b) WE ARE NOT INDEPENDENT:] We are not independent of [Client].
> [Optional, if disclosing reasons:] Our lack of independence
> arises from [REASONS — disclose ALL substantive reasons].
> Our compilation report will include a paragraph disclosing our
> lack of independence as required by AR-C §80.27.
>
> **Management's responsibilities**
>
> Management is responsible for:
> - The financial statements and their fair presentation.
> - Selection and application of accounting policies under the
>   framework.
> - Designing, implementing, and maintaining internal control.
> - Preventing and detecting fraud.
> - Providing all financial records, information, and personnel
>   to us.
>
> **Form of report**
>
> Upon completion, we will issue a written compilation report
> containing the standard compilation language [plus the
> independence-disclosure paragraph if applicable].
>
> **Going concern**
>
> If conditions or events come to our attention that raise
> substantial doubt about the entity's ability to continue as a
> going concern, and management has not addressed them in the
> financial statements, we will request management to consider
> the matter and reflect it as required by [framework]. If
> management does not, we may modify the compilation report or
> withdraw.
>
> **Departures from framework**
>
> If we become aware of a known departure from [framework] that
> is material, we will propose revisions. If management does not
> revise, we will modify the compilation report by adding a
> paragraph that discloses the departure and its effects (if
> known).
>
> **Fees, termination, other terms**
>
> [SEE COMMON-CLAUSES TEMPLATE]
>
> **Indemnification limitation**
>
> The firm's liability is governed by applicable law and AICPA
> Ethics Interpretation §1.400.205 (where applicable to the
> compilation). Indemnification provisions favoring the firm for
> the firm's own acts are not included.
>
> Please sign to indicate your agreement.
>
> [PARTNER NAME / FIRM NAME]
>
> Acknowledged: [CLIENT / TITLE / DATE]

## Practitioner notes

- Compilation is the only SSARS engagement that permits
  performance without independence (with disclosure).
- The lack-of-independence disclosure paragraph is required
  if the firm is not independent (AR-C §80.27).
- The firm has options for disclosing reasons:
  - Disclose all reasons.
  - Disclose no reasons (just the fact).
  - Disclose specific reasons but not all (must disclose ALL
    substantive reasons if any are disclosed).
- Special-purpose framework requires additional report language
  per AR-C §80.A33.

## Authority

- AR-C §60 (general principles), §80 (compilation): primary.
- AR-C §80.27 (lack-of-independence disclosure): for
  non-independent compilation.
- AR-C §80.A24 (going concern), §80.A29 (departure from
  framework), §80.A33 (special-purpose framework).
- AICPA Code §1.295 (nonattest services scoping that may have
  led to lack of independence).
