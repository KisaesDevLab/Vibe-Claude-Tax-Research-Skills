# SOC 2 engagement letter (AT-C §205 + Trust Services Criteria)

For Service Organization Control 2 (SOC 2) examinations on
controls relevant to AICPA Trust Services Criteria.

## Required elements

- [ ] Service organization name and system description scope.
- [ ] Report type: Type 1 or Type 2.
- [ ] Reporting period (Type 2).
- [ ] Trust Services Categories selected (Security mandatory;
      Availability / Processing Integrity / Confidentiality /
      Privacy optional).
- [ ] Subservice organization treatment: carve-out or inclusive.
- [ ] CUEC identification.
- [ ] Independence affirmation.
- [ ] Use restriction language.

>

> - [Optional: **Processing Integrity** — system processing is
>   complete, valid, accurate, timely, authorized].
> - [Optional: **Confidentiality** — information designated as
>   confidential is protected].
> - [Optional: **Privacy** — personal information is collected,
>   used, retained, disclosed, disposed of in accordance with

>
> **System description**
>

> will cover [SCOPE — services, infrastructure, software, people,
> procedures, data].
>

>
> Our engagement is performed in accordance with AT-C §205

> Our procedures will provide reasonable assurance.
>

>
> Subservice organizations identified: [LIST].
>
> Treatment: [CARVE-OUT or INCLUSIVE].
>
> [If carve-out: CSOCs (complementary subservice organization

>

>

>
> **Management responsibilities**
>
> Service organization management is responsible for:
> - The description of the system.
> - The selection of trust services categories.
> - The design and implementation of controls to meet the

## Practitioner notes

- Security is the only mandatory trust-services category.
- Privacy is the broadest in scope and triggers state, federal,
  and international privacy-law considerations.
- SOC 2 typically restricted-use; SOC 3 is the general-use
  alternative for marketing.
- Mapping to NIST CSF / ISO 27001 is value-add but not required.
- Cybersecurity Risk Management Reporting Framework (separate
  AICPA) is NOT a SOC 2 alternative.
