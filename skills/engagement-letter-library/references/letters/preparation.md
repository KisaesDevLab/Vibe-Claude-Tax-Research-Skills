# Preparation engagement letter (AR-C §70.10)

Required elements per AR-C §60.A21 + AR-C §70.10. NO assurance,
NO report. "No assurance is provided" legend required on each
page of F/S.

## Required AICPA elements

- [ ] Statement that engagement is to prepare F/S and is in
      accordance with SSARS.
- [ ] Statement that practitioner is NOT required to verify
      accuracy or completeness.
- [ ] Notation that NO assurance is provided.
- [ ] Statement that "no assurance is provided" legend will
      appear on each page (or that a disclaimer will accompany
      F/S in lieu of the per-page legend).
- [ ] Identification of framework.
- [ ] Management responsibilities.
- [ ] Fee, termination, communication.

## Template

> **PREPARATION ENGAGEMENT LETTER**
>
> [DATE]
>
> [CLIENT NAME]
> [CLIENT ADDRESS]
>
> We are pleased to confirm our understanding of the terms of
> our engagement to prepare the financial statements of [CLIENT
> NAME] for the [PERIOD/YEAR] ending [DATE].
>
> **Engagement**
>
> Our engagement is to prepare financial statements that comprise
> [LIST] in accordance with [framework].
>
> Our engagement will be performed in accordance with Statements
> on Standards for Accounting and Review Services (SSARS) issued
> by the AICPA, specifically AR-C §70 — Preparation of Financial
> Statements. We will NOT issue a compilation, review, or audit
> report; NO assurance is provided.
>
> Each page of the financial statements will include a statement
> indicating that no assurance is provided on the financial
> statements (e.g., "These financial statements have not been
> subjected to an audit, review, or compilation engagement, and
> no assurance is provided."). [Or: in lieu of the per-page
> legend, we will issue a disclaimer that accompanies the F/S.]
>
> **Independence**
>
> Independence is not required for a preparation engagement.
>
> **Management's responsibilities**
>
> Management is responsible for:
> - The financial statements.
> - Selection and application of accounting policies under the
>   framework.
> - Designing, implementing, and maintaining internal control.
> - Preventing and detecting fraud.
> - Compliance with laws and regulations.
> - Providing all records, information, and personnel.
>
> We are NOT required to verify accuracy or completeness. If the
> information provided to us is incorrect, incomplete, or
> otherwise unsatisfactory, we will request management to
> provide additional or corrected information. If management does
> not provide it, we will withdraw or modify the work product.
>
> **Departure from framework**
>
> If we become aware of a known material departure from
> [framework], we will propose appropriate revisions. If
> management refuses, we will disclose the departure on the
> face of the F/S, withdraw, or change the engagement to a
> compilation with an appropriate compilation report.
>
> **Fees, termination, other terms**
>
> [SEE COMMON-CLAUSES TEMPLATE]
>
> Please sign to indicate your agreement.
>
> [PARTNER NAME / FIRM NAME]
>
> Acknowledged: [CLIENT / TITLE / DATE]

## Practitioner notes

- AR-C §70 is the lowest-service tier under SSARS.
- NO report is issued. The "no assurance is provided" legend on
  each page is the only required communication.
- If the client expects a report, route to AR-C §80
  (compilation).
- If the client expects assurance, route to AR-C §90 (review)
  or AU-C (audit).
- Independence is NOT required.

## Authority

- AR-C §60 (general principles), §70 (preparation): primary.
- AR-C §70.14 (no-assurance legend), §70.A19 (departure from
  framework), §70.A21 (specified elements).
