## Estimated tax (§6654 / §6655)

### Individual (calendar year)

| Quarter | Period | Deadline |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | January 1 – March 31 | April 15 |
| Q2 | April 1 – May 31 | June 15 |
| Q3 | June 1 – August 31 | September 15 |
| Q4 | September 1 – December 31 | January 15 of following year |

§6654(g) — withholding from wages or pensions is treated as paid
EVENLY across the year, regardless of when withheld. End-of-year
withholding from RMD or year-end bonus can therefore satisfy a
prior-quarter shortfall.

### Corporate (calendar year)

| Quarter | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Q1 | April 15 |
| Q2 | June 15 |
| Q3 | September 15 |
| Q4 | December 15 |

### §6654(d)(1)(B) safe harbors

- 90% of current-year tax; OR
- 100% of prior-year tax (110% if AGI > $150K).

## Payroll deposits

§6302 deposit schedules:
- Monthly schedule depositor: deposit by 15th of following month.
- Semi-weekly schedule depositor: deposits within 3 banking days
  of payday for Wed/Thu/Fri payrolls; within 3 banking days for
  Sat-Tue payrolls.
- $100,000 next-day rule: any single payroll's tax liability ≥
  $100K must be deposited next banking day.

Schedule determined by lookback period (12-month period ending
prior June 30):
- ≤ $50K total liability → monthly.
- > $50K total liability → semi-weekly.

§6651(a)(3) failure-to-deposit penalty: 2% / 5% / 10% / 15%
based on lateness.

## §7503 weekend / holiday adjustment

If deadline falls on Saturday, Sunday, or DC legal holiday,
extended to next business day.

Federal holidays:
- January 1 (New Year's Day).
- 3rd Monday in January (MLK Day).
- 3rd Monday in February (Presidents' Day).
- Last Monday in May (Memorial Day).
- June 19 (Juneteenth — since 2021).
- July 4 (Independence Day).
- 1st Monday in September (Labor Day).
- 2nd Monday in October (Columbus Day).
- November 11 (Veterans Day).
- 4th Thursday in November (Thanksgiving).
- December 25 (Christmas).

DC-only Emancipation Day (April 16) treats April 16 as a federal
holiday for IRS purposes, often pushing Tax Day past April 15
when April 15 is a weekend.

Examples:
- 2023: April 15 Saturday, April 17 was Emancipation Day observed
  in DC, so Tax Day was April 18.
- 2024: April 15 Monday, no holiday issue.
- 2025: April 15 Tuesday, no holiday issue.

## §7508A disaster extensions

For taxpayers in IRS-designated disaster areas, the IRS
periodically extends deadlines via news release. Verify against
IRS newsroom announcements for the specific disaster.

## §6213 Tax Court 90-day deadline

90 days from statutory notice of deficiency (150 days if mailed
outside U.S.). JURISDICTIONAL absent equitable tolling under
Boechler / Hallmark Research Collective frameworks.

## §6320 / §6330 CDP 30-day deadline

30 days from notice of lien filing (Letter 3172) or final notice
of intent to levy (Letter 1058 / CP90 / LT11).

## §6511 refund-claim deadline

§6511(a) — LATER of:
- 3 years from filing; OR
- 2 years from payment.

§6511(b)(2) — refund limited to tax paid within applicable
look-back window.

## §6411 tentative refund

Form 1045 (individuals) or Form 1139 (corporations): within 12
months of close of loss year.

## Quick-reference: common scenarios

### Multi-state remote worker, calendar 2024 1040

- Federal: April 15, 2025.
- Federal extension: October 15, 2025 (Form 4868).
- State variations: per `tax-research-state-income/<XX>` per-state
  file.

### Calendar 2024 partnership / S-corp

- 1065 / 1120-S: March 15, 2025.
- 1065 / 1120-S extension: September 15, 2025 (Form 7004).
- K-1s to partners / shareholders: same as primary return.

### 2024 estate (Form 706)

- 9 months from date of death.
- 6-month extension via Form 4768.

### 2024 gift (Form 709)

- April 15, 2025.
- Extension via Form 4868 (auto for 1040 filers) or Form 8892
  (separate).
